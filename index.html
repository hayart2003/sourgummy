History of the font

Stefie Justprince created the font Sour Gummy in 2018. The font is both fun and exciting. Not only does this typeface showcase a cheerful look, but Justprince designed the font to mimic the look of bubbles. Sour Gummy has been updated to offer numbers, lower and uppercase letters, and punctuation. Justprince created the typeface with three variable axes: italics, width, and height. In addition to Sour Gummy, Justprince created a typeface called Pixelify Sans.
