History of the font

Stefie Justprince created Sour Gummy in 2018. It is fun and exciting. Not only does this typeface showcase a cheerful look, but Just Prince also designed the font to mimic the bubbles' look. Sour Gummy has been updated to offer numbers, lower and uppercase letters, and punctuation. Just Prince created the typeface with three axes, in italics, and various sizes. In addition to Sour Gummy, Justprince created another typeface called Pixelify Sans.
